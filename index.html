About Us

Euna Engineering Services Pvt. Ltd. is a private, independent firm based in Pokhara, Nepal established in 2074 B.S. A group of experienced and dedicated Engineers and professionals initiate the organization to provide technical assistant and professional services within the field of Civil, Electronics, Mechanical engineering and new suitable techniques required for the country.

Nepal is listed as one among five least developed countries of the world by the recent world development report published by the World Bank. Because of its geographical location along the Himalayas believed by geologist to be the mountain, it has a fragile environment and a Unique Ecology having lack of specialized and highly skilled professionals.

In this scenario, the overall goal set by the Euna Engineering Services Pvt. Ltd. is to make maximum of its effort in order to contribute to the development activities within the country with overall improved technical aspects.

Main objectives of this firm are planning, survey, design, supervision, fixed asset property valuation, motor valuation, insurance assessment and management of the project within all fields of engineering and related science.

From the outset, our primary concern has always been to render professional and personal services of the highest quality to our client who includes government, semi-government, non-government and private enterprises.
